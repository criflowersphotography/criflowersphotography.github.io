About Me

about me

...IN FEW WORDS

I'm Cristian Fiori, an IT consultant with the passion of photography and videography discovered in 2018.
I currently work with Più o Meno Pop, DafenProject, Distopic, for what concern concert photography; with PhotoToday, OTC for sports photography.
I shooted for Home Venice during Fuori Salone 2019 in Milan and I worked with HT Factory venue for their gigs.

FROM THE BEGINNING TO NOWADAYS

After a couple of years, spent by learning and taking amatorial photos, in 2018 I decided to open a Facebook business page and an Instagram profile, more impactful and immediate especially for photos, just to share in public my shots.
I started shooting local bands in a pub called Honky Tonky, now HT Factory, for moving me then into Distopic, a web magazine focused on concerts and news about that.
Since 2019 I work also for two others webzine called Più o Meno Pop and DafenProject, which have the same orientation.
Lastly I took part into two web magazines focused on sports called PhotoToday and OTC.

In my career I took pictures at national and International artists like NOFX, Sick Of It All, Ska-p, Anti-Flag, Millencolin, Danko Jones, Salmo, Ultimo, Subsonica, Pennywise, Good Riddance, Less Than Jake, Frank Turner, Finley, Hot Snakes, Saor Patrol, Carl Brave, Davide Petrella, Alborosie, Franco 126, Tre Allegri Ragazzi Morti, Punkreas and many many more...
