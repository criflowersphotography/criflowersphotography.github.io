About Me

about me

...IN FEW WORDS

I'm Cristian Fiori, an IT consultant with passion of photography and videography discovered in 2018.
I'm the official photographer/videomaker of Groove Temple.
I'm working with Più o Meno Pop, DafenProject and Distopic for everything related to concert photography and with PhotoToday and OTC for sports photography.
I shooted for Home Venice during Fuori Salone 2019 event in Milan and I worked with HT Factory venue for their gigs.

FROM THE BEGINNING TO NOWADAYS

After a couple of years, spent by learning and taking amatorial photos, in 2018 I decided to open a Facebook business page and an Instagram profile, more impactful and immediate especially for photos, to share my best photos.
I started shooting local bands in a pub called Honky Tonky, now HT Factory, then I went into Distopic, a web magazine focused on concerts and news about that.
Since 2019 I've been working also for two webzine called Più o Meno Pop and DafenProject, which have the same orientation.
Lastly I took part into two web magazines focused on sports called PhotoToday and OTC.

In my career I took pictures at national and International artists like NOFX, Sick Of It All, Ska-p, Anti-Flag, Millencolin, Danko Jones, Salmo, Ultimo, Subsonica, Pennywise, Good Riddance, Less Than Jake, Frank Turner, Finley, Hot Snakes, Saor Patrol, Carl Brave, Davide Petrella, Alborosie, Franco 126, Tre Allegri Ragazzi Morti, Punkreas and many many more...
